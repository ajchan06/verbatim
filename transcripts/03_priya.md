ALEX: Hi Priya, thanks for joining. Can you hear me okay?

PRIYA: Yes I can hear you. I'm in my car actually so if I cut out that's why.

ALEX: No problem. We can keep it short. So, you signed up for Linkup and cancelled after a few days. I just want to understand what happened from your side.

PRIYA: Okay so I'm a real estate agent. I'm in my car between showings probably six hours a day. I needed something where I could reschedule showings on the fly, send links from my phone, that kind of thing. The web app, when I tried it on my laptop, it was fine. It was good actually. But the mobile app — I can't.

ALEX: What was hard about the mobile app?

PRIYA: Where do I start. So first, half the things I can do on web I can't do on mobile. I couldn't edit my event types from the phone. I had to pull over and use my laptop one time which is the opposite of what this should be doing for me. Second, it's slow. Like genuinely slow, like I'd tap something and there'd be a spinner for five seconds. And third, the UI is just — it feels like nobody tested it on a phone. Buttons too small, text gets cut off, my booking link wouldn't copy properly half the time.

ALEX: Wow, okay. Did you reach out to support?

PRIYA: No, I didn't. I figured if the mobile app is this bad, this is probably a known thing. Either they don't care about mobile or they're working on it and it's not ready. Either way, not my problem to figure out.

ALEX: That's fair. What are you using now?

PRIYA: Calendly's mobile is fine. Not amazing but it works. I switched back.

ALEX: If the mobile experience were as good as web, would you have stayed?

PRIYA: Yes. Probably. The web app I really liked actually. There were these little touches like the way it shows you a heat map of your week, that was cool. But I do ninety percent of my work from my phone. So it doesn't matter how nice the web is.

ALEX: Got it. Anything else you want me to know?

PRIYA: Just — please tell whoever builds the mobile app that real estate agents exist. And salespeople, and anyone who isn't sitting at a desk. We're not a small group.

ALEX: I will pass that along. Thank you Priya.

PRIYA: You got it.
