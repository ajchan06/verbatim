ALEX: Rafael, thanks for the time. I know you've been talking to our AE about a larger contract.

RAFAEL: Yeah, we're trying to figure out if we expand from our current five seats to forty. Big jump. So this is good timing.

ALEX: What's pushing the expansion conversation?

RAFAEL: The five people who have it love it. Our sales team has been begging me to roll it out to all the AEs. Round-robin is the main draw, honestly. Right now only our enterprise team uses it and they want every AE on it.

ALEX: What's holding you back from just doing it?

RAFAEL: Two things really. First is the Salesforce integration. We're a Salesforce shop, like every workflow we have runs through it. And from what I've heard from the enterprise team, the Salesforce sync is unreliable. Drops bookings, attributes things wrong, sometimes the activity doesn't log at all. If I'm rolling this out to forty people, I can't have that. The data integrity problem becomes forty times worse.

ALEX: How recent is that feedback?

RAFAEL: Last week I sat down with our enterprise team lead and she walked me through three examples of broken syncs from the past month. So yeah, it's current. I think you all know about it, my AE has mentioned a fix is in flight.

ALEX: What's the second thing?

RAFAEL: Provisioning. To set up a new user takes a non-trivial amount of work. SCIM would help, we use Okta for identity, and I think you support SCIM but it's still — there's a per-user setup step in Linkup that I have to do or coordinate. For five users, fine. For forty, I'd want it more automated.

ALEX: That's useful. Any pricing considerations?

RAFAEL: Forty seats at twelve a head is a real line item. Five hundred and something a month. I can justify it but I'm going to need to negotiate. My AE knows that.

ALEX: If both of those things were solved — Salesforce reliable, provisioning automated — what's your timeline?

RAFAEL: Honestly, two weeks. I'd push it through procurement. We're ready.

ALEX: Got it. What if you had to put a number on the percentage chance you expand this quarter, given current state?

RAFAEL: Maybe forty percent. If the Salesforce situation gets a real timeline I can show my CRO, it goes up to seventy.

ALEX: That's incredibly clear, thank you. Anything else you want me to know?

RAFAEL: The product is good. The round-robin is the best I've seen. Just don't lose us on the integration story. We're rooting for you all.

ALEX: Appreciate that, Rafael. Thanks.

RAFAEL: Anytime.
